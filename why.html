Our Process

We'll be with you every step of the way.

How does Implementation work?

We assign an implementation specialist that works with you to understand your business and configure the solutions for the capabilities that maximize your business impact. For our HR Services our certified experts run an HR Audit on your organization with your help so that we can recommended and tailor a program specific to your needs.

Does company size matter?

Our typical client has 5,000 or fewer employees although we are expanding to larger organizations with our Wellness and HR Services. That being said, what matters most isn’t your size, but what you’re trying to achieve. If your organizations belief is developing its people and providing the best possible employment experience, then you will love us.

Will my employees use the software?

One of the top concerns we hear is around employee usage. It turns out 82% of employees on the platform log in on a regular basis. Further, your employees will have easy access and all their information at their fingertips, anywhere, anytime by mobile or web.

How will we benefit from switching?

The average HR administrator saves 7 hours per week. Once your HR, Payroll and Benefits are in sync, you’ll have more time to focus on engagement, retention, strategy, and culture.

How our support works

Once you’re fully implemented, the support just begins. Our talented Client Experience team will be your guardian angels and extension of your team ensuring that you and your team are maximizing the value of our powerful software and services. Your assigned dedicated Account Manager will know your business and be available to help.

How to approach leaders about switching

Big decision require multiple stakeholders, each with their own set of priorities. We’ll help provide you the information you need to make the best decision for your business that you can share with others and introduce you to our existing clients who will be happy to share their experiences.
