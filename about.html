Our Story

We Believe People Make The Difference

MassPay was founded in 2004 to provide small and mid-sized companies a partner who understood their business and alleviate the stress that they were experiencing with publicly traded payroll companies. Since then, we’ve extended our offerings to include HR and Wellness software and solutions for business small to large and local to international. We now help our clients attract and retain great people, navigate a constantly changing world of regulation requirements, and engage their employees. Every step of the way, we have stayed true to our WHY…… WE BELIEVE PEOPLE MAKE THE DIFFERENCE.

Making The Difference
