The Process

From first contact all the way to hired, we're with you along the way.

01

Attract

Partner with a dedicated HR Recruiter who will work with you on the job description, compensation data, qualifying questions, and get the job posted for you.

02

Qualify

As candidates apply, they are determined to be qualified or unqualified. Qualified candidates will be sent a video interview.

03

Assess

An Online Assessment is sent to each candidate that has completed an interview.

04

Updates

From the date the position is posted, the HR Recruiter sends weekly updates via email on activity from the position.

05

Results

After 3 weeks, our clients receive a link providing them access to all content for their position with candidates resumes, automated interviews completed, and assessments completed.

06

Hire

Based on returned information from candidates, the assigned HR Recruiter will provide the client with recommendations for next steps. Clients can move forward quickly towards hiring with detailed candidate profiles.
